  issue). `rm` is blocked on lockfiles by the FUSE mount; moved the
  file out of the way with `mv .git/index.lock .git/index.lock.stale-$$`

  the sandbox can't reach ‚Äî same recurring egress blocker. Switched to

  `node_modules` vs the Linux sandbox) ‚Äî fixed with a local
